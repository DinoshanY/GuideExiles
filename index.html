Class

There are 6 classes you can pick form the start and 1 unlockabel class before a boss battle in chapter 4.

Each class specialisel in diffrent or combind atributes: Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence.
You can pick from Marauder: Strength, Ranger: Dexterity, Witch: Intelligence, Duelist: Strength & Dexterity, Shadow: Dexterity & Intelligence , Templar: Intelligence & Strength, and finally the unlockabel class Scion: All Atributes. each class has also three ascendancy exepte Scion she has one that is a mix of every ascendancy. Ascendancy is a special version of a class with uniqe passives. The classes have diffrent staring point in the passive tree. Strength classes have alot of melee passives nodes, Intelligence has alot of spell passives nodes and Dexterity has alot of range and trap passives nodes. When picking a class it is importent to have fun so pick a class that will make you enjoy the game.

Defences

There are a lot of different armor types in Path Of Exiles and you can use mulitbel types of defences at the same time. The four main defencess are Armor, Evasion, Energy shield and Ward. If an item has the discribison defences or globel defences it means all the main defences exept Ward.

Armor reduce physical damage down and the cap is 90%.

Evasion gives a chance to evade all physical attacks and ranged attacks the cap is 95%.

Energy Shield, most players think of energy shield as part of there health and some builds use energy shield as there main defences but energy shield builds are not beginner friendly so it is not recemende as a starter build.

Ward, Takes the remaing damages after reduction from different effekts and it reduce one hit and starts recharging for 5 secound before it can be used, If you take 0 damage I wont activate. Ps. Nobody uses Ward exepte meme builds.

Resistens, There are only four types of resistens Elementel Resistens and Chaos Resistens. The normale maxium resistens is 75% for each type and the cap can be increasd to a 90%-100% form what I have seen. Your resisten reduceds the damage taken before all your other defences. If you have 75% fire resistens and take 100 fire damage you will only take 25 fire damage. It is possibel to become Chaos Immune but you will only have 1 HP and MOST run a Energy shield build.

Block, There are to types of blocks Physicall and spell block chance the cap is 75% butt can be increasd with gems or items, when blocking you take 0% of the damage but if you pick the passive tree node Glancing Blows you take 65% damage you block but you attack and spell block chanse is doubled. If a projectiles is a spell I wont be blocked expted if you have a special item for spell projectiles.

Spell Dodge, Is chance to dodge spell from hitting with a cap at 75%.

Spell suppression, Is chance to take half of spell damage the cap is 100%.
