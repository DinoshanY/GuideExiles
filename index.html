Class

There are 6 classes you can pick form the start and 1 unlockabel class before a boss battle in chapter 4.

Each class specialisel in diffrent or combind atributes: Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence. You can pick from Marauder: Strength, Ranger: Dexterity, Witch: Intelligence, Duelist: Strength & Dexterity, Shadow: Dexterity & Intelligence , Templar: Intelligence & Strength, and finally the unlockabel class Scion: All Atributes. each class has also three ascendancy exepte Scion she has one that is a mix of every ascendancy. Ascendancy is a special version of a class with uniqe passives. The classes have diffrent staring point in the passive tree. Strength classes have alot of melee passives nodes, Intelligence has alot of spell passives nodes and Dexterity has alot of range and trap passives nodes. When picking a class it is importent to have fun so pick a class that will make you enjoy the game.

On the bottom right you see all your key binds for diffrent skills and if you hold Ctrl you will see 5 more solts. if you right click the weapon icon you will see two option i recemend putting the feet icon on left click and the weapon icon on right, later when you get your first skill place it on the right click. then Talk to the man sitting.

After talking to him a enemie will kill him and attack you. you need to kill the enemie beacuse it drops a skill gem, pick up the gem and press I to open the inventory chosse the gem from your inventory and then the sockets on the weapon will ligth up, place the gem in the socket and you will get a skill. Then follow the map to the rigth til you find a create.
rigth click the create and a support skill will drop, pick it up and open the inventory, place the gem in the open socket linked to the skill gem. Gems

There are 4 gems colors: Red, Green, Blue and White. the color of the socket on your gear can only use the same color gem, Exepte white it can be placed in all socket nom matter the color, A white gem can be placed in any socket and a all gems can be placed in a white socket. Almost all gem cost mana or life but som gems reserve mana or life to give a buffing aura to the player. The max level and quality of gems are 20 but can become higher by corrupting the gem. Quality give a small buff to gems, And a corrupted gem is the same as a normale gem exepte it cant be change after corruptison.

There are alot of diffrent types og gems, skills, support, awakeand and vaal gems, awakeand gems are better den normale gems but not every gem has en awkaend veriant they also take longer to level up. Vaal gems are the same as corrupted gems but have a special gem effect that cost the enemies you kill insted of mana or life. Some gems have a diffrent version of them self were quality gives the gem a diffrent small buff.

In the picture you see all 19 ascendancy, they are not on the passive tree. only the one ascendancy you pick can you see and use.

Cluster jewel is basical a extension to the passive tree. They give diffrent passives and uniqe passive that you use your passive skill points to unlock.
